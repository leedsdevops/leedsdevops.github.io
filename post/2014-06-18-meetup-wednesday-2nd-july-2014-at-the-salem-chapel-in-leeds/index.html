Meetup - Wednesday 2nd July 2014 at The Salem Chapel in Leeds

We've managed to find a date and venue sandwiched between World Cup fixtures. Our next Devops meetup will take place at 7pm on Wednesday 2nd July at the home of AQL in Leeds' coolest venue The Salem Chapel

Posted by LeedsDevops on Wed, Jun 18, 2014
In Meetups
Tags leeds, devops, meetup

We’ve managed to find a date and venue sandwiched between World Cup fixtures. Our next Devops meetup will take place at 7pm on Wednesday 2nd July at the home of AQL in Leeds’ coolest venue The Salem Chapel

We have two great speakers for you:

  • Peter Maude - CTO of Leeds Based Crisp Thinking, they provide a service that takes care of moderating and managing User Generated Content, enabling customers to focus on delivering a social experience.

    This is a few years old, but explains the principles….



    Peter will be telling us all about how Crisp was designed, built and managed to provide a social media moderation platform for some household names.
  • Stephen Myers - Head of .NET development at award winning full service digital agency twentysix, with over 15 years experience at the sharp end of web development and delivery, I’ll be talking about the tools we use in the agency, as well as looking at the latest devops tools for the .NET developer ecosystem.

The venue is situated across the road from the Adelphi it’s a doddle to find, in addition to the venue being part auditorium part server room there will as always there’s a well stocked bar.

Tickets available from eventbrite and meetup.com
