In Meetups

So with well over a hundred and thirty twitter followers it's time to start thinking of our first meetup, So like a couple moving into a starter home, we should say hello to the neighbours....

  • Leeds Php meet 3rd Monday of each month - http://leedsphp.org/
  • Leeds Ruby Thing meet the last Thursday of the month - http://lanyrd.com/guides/leeds-ruby-thing/
  • Node Up North - meet the last Wednesday of the month - http://www.meetup.com/NodeUpNorth/
  • Leeds JS seem to be hibernating at the moment - http://javascript-leeds.tumblr.com/
  • Agile Yorkshire meet the 2nd Tuesday of the month - http://www.agileyorkshire.org/
  • Drupal Yorkshire meet 2nd tuesday of the month - http://drupalyorkshire.org.uk/
  • WY Python meet 2nd Thursday of the month - https://twitter.com/WYPython
  • Leeds Data Thing meets every couple of months - http://www.leedsdatathing.co.uk/
  • Leeds Sharp meets last Thursday of the month - http://www.leeds-sharp.org/
  • WYLUG meets 2nd Monday of the month - http://www.wylug.org.uk/
  • Leeds Hackspace meets every Tuesday - http://leeds.hackspace.org.uk/
  • Northern Bloggers and WordPress Leeds meets 1st Wednesday of the month - http://wpleeds.co.uk/

We reckon a good choice would be the second Monday of the month, so keep Monday the 14th October free - more news when we have a venue.
