Resources for the Agile Yorkshire Group

We presented a 'DevOps 101' talk at Agile Yorkshire last week. One of the slides listed resources for learning more about DevOps and I promised to create a&nbsp;page on our site listing them in detail.

Posted by LeedsDevops on Sat, Feb 15, 2014
In Meetups
Tags devops, resources

We presented a "DevOps 101" talk at Agile Yorkshire last week. One of the slides listed resources for learning more about DevOps and I promised to create a page on our site listing them in detail. Here it is....

Podcasts

  • Devops Cafe - http://devopscafe.org/ - Original DevOps podcast, still current and relevant, older shows include discussions with all the names that matter in DevOps
  • Foodfight Show - http://foodfightshow.org/ chef focused discussion on devops
  • Arrested DevOps - http://www.arresteddevops.com/ new podcast

 

Twitter

Search for the #devops hastag

  • @patrickdebois: Patrick Debois - the godfather of DevOps.
  • @RealGeneKim: author of "The Pheonix Project" the must read DevOps novel.
  • @botchagalupe: John Willis - devops cafe podcast host, credited with creating the CAMS definition, huge fan of Demming.
  • @damonedwards: Damon Edwards - the other half of the devops cafe podcast team.
  • @jezhumble: Jez Humble - Continuous Delivery guru
  • @bryanwb: Byan Berry - host of foodfight show, works with chef and cloud infrastructure
  • @nathenharvey: Nathan Harvey - Technical Community Manager at Chef and host of foodfight show
  • @jordansissel: Jordan Sissel - devops comentator and author of logstash
  • @garethr: Gareth Rushgrove - curator of DevOps weekly
  • @lusis: John E. Vincent - proponent of anger driven devlopment
  • @DEVOPS_BORAT: DevOps Borat - devops wisdom

Puppet Labs also maintain a list of DevOps accouts to follow https://twitter.com/puppetlabs/lists/devops-accounts
